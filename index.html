Why your loan rate matters more than you think

Your loan interest rate is a guaranteed return — every rupee you prepay earns exactly that rate, risk-free. At 9% p.a., prepaying a home loan beats an FD (7%), most bonds, and matches conservative equity returns over shorter horizons.

How to negotiate a lower rate with your bank

Ask your bank for a "rate reset" to the current EBLR + spread. If they refuse, a balance transfer to a competing bank often cuts 0.5–1.0% — same effect as a significant lump-sum prepayment.
